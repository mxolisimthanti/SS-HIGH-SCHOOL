Academic Excellence at Sigweje High School

At Sigweje High School, teaching and learning are at the heart of everything we do. While we value sport and culture, our primary focus remains on delivering quality public education to every learner. We are proud of our tradition of academic excellence and remain committed to nurturing future-ready students.

Our academic ethos encourages independent thinking, curiosity, and disciplined study habits. Learners are empowered to take ownership of their education, preparing them for tertiary studies and life beyond school. Many of our students excel in academic competitions such as Maths, Science, and Accounting Olympiads, regularly earning top placements nationally.

Curriculum Overview

In Grades 8 and 9, learners engage with all eight learning areas, with a language choice between Afrikaans and isiZulu as a First Additional Language. Our Arts and Culture program introduces Music and Visual Arts, which can be pursued further in the FET phase.

From Grade 10 to 12, learners follow a conventional academic path, selecting subjects that align with their strengths and interests. Our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire, with high expectations reflected in our learners’ achievements in public exams and competitions.

Subject Choices

Grades 10–12

  • Compulsory Subjects:
    • English Home Language
    • First Additional Language (Afrikaans or isiZulu)
    • Mathematics or Mathematical Literacy

  • Elective Subjects (Choose 3):
    • Physical Science
    • Life Science
    • Business Studies
    • Geography
    • Accounting
    • Information Technology
    • Computer Applications Technology
    • Engineering Graphics and Design
    • Visual Arts
    • Consumer Studies

Grades 8–9 (GET Phase)

  • Social Science
  • Life Orientation
  • Mathematics
  • Natural Science
  • Technology
  • English Home Language
  • First Additional Language (Afrikaans or isiZulu)
  • Creative Arts
  • Economic & Management Sciences (EMS)

Top Ten Club

The Sigweje High School Top Ten Club celebrates academic excellence across all grades. Established to recognize our highest achievers, the club motivates learners to strive for consistent performance and personal growth. Each term, the top ten learners from Grades 8 to 12 are honored for their dedication and results.
